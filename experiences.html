My online learning experiences during lockdown...

My online learning experiences is completely different than what I am used to do in physical class. To be a successful distance learner, I have noticed that you must be self-disciplined and capable of working independently. Since you won't be physically attending class each week, you can participate in it from the convenience of your own home and must be self-disciplined because the instructor will not continually remind you of tasks or project deadlines.

Thus, being able to speak with the lecturers and other students in these classes has helped me become a more active participant in the classes. I have ve never found it difficult to reach out to my lecturers for assistance or clarification on an assignment. All of these lecturerss, as well as the individuals in these programmes, have been really helpful. I have also learnt that discussing one other's perspectives forces us to analyse our own thoughts and feelings. This makes class work more fascinating because it allows us to share personal experiences while also developing our writing skills.
